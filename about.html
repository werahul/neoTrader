A trailblazer in Indian trading, pioneering various initiatives: Premier Technical Analysis Advisory Firm; 'Trend Trader', India's oldest newsletter; Providing Training at UTI & BSE; Sole Technical Analysis Magazine 'Capital Market Technicals'; Early Technical Analysis Website 'ChartAdvise'; Advising Domestic and FII Funds; Full-service Firm 'Growth Avenues'. Honored with ATMA's Lifetime Achievement Award.
NeoTrader was born as India's finest analysis and trading software. Its journey traces back to the early days when Dr. C.K. Narayan, a dentist turned stock trader in 1978, discovered his passion for the stock market. Fast forward to today, NeoTrader has become the ultimate trading companion, offering an array of features to make trading wiser, more efficient, and accessible to all.
NeoTrader expanded its reach this year, showcasing its prowess in analysis and trading software through extensive nationwide events, seminars, and conferences. This outreach aimed to educate and empower individuals across the country, marking a significant milestone in its mission to make profitable trading accessible to all.
NeoTrader achieved a significant milestone by integrating with leading brokers such as Zerodha and Alice Blue. This strategic move propelled NeoTrader to victory in the Online Startup Championship, solidifying its position as a cutting-edge trading platform.
NeoTrader is been recognized as the best trading software by Alice Blue. This accolade further solidified NeoTrader's reputation as India's premier analysis and trading software, a culmination of Dr. C.K. Narayan's extensive experience and the collaborative efforts of the expert trio.
NeoTrader reached new heights with the introduction of high-powered features incorporating CPR, RSI, ATR, and ADX. These additions further enhanced the platform, providing traders with advanced tools for analysis and decision-making, reinforcing its commitment to empowering individuals in their trading journey.
NeoTrader achieved notable recognition and formed strategic partnerships with FYERS, DHAN, and 5 Paisa. The platform garnered awards, solidifying its position as a leading trading software. Dr. C.K. Narayan, Raja Venkatraman, and Aditya Iyer were invited as key speakers at prominent broker events and conferences, showcasing NeoTrader's impact on the trading community.
